## Questions

1. Compare offset vs cursor pagination; when is cursor safer?
2. What query params would you use for sorting a list safely?

---

## Answers

1. Offset is simple but unstable, not reliable and performant for large database.

Cursor is fast and stable, especially for dynamic changing data. 

2. Define a sort field eg. sort=createdAt,id to sort by created time first and id second.

Also define the direction field called ordet eg. order=asc or order=desc.
